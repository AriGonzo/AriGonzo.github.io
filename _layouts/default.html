About Me

A lifelong technophile and a product of the generation that straddles Wikipedia and Dewey Decimal. Netflix and Blockbuster Video. With a strong love and respect for technology and the internet, I took the plunge and immersed myself in all things coding by attending The Iron Yard Academy's Orlando Front-End Engineering cohort. Now that I have completed that course, I'm ready to begin my career in development.

In a former life, I was a Project Manager in the field of Event Management. Traveling the US putting on events in different cities for companies of all sizes. I've been fortunate enough to have seen many amazing places and had a ton of experiences that have made me who I am today. I like to put the spin of my life experiences into anything I work on so that my work is always uniquely me.

The Mighty DavidBot!

The result of The Iron Yard instructor David Rogers' assignment of 'Make something interesting using at least 2 public APIs.' This chrome extension will answer any question you ask it similar to David's method: it will take your question and send you to 'LetMeGoogleThatForYou.com.' Find something interesting? Click on the chat icon and your current open tab URL will be dropped into the class Hipchat room to share the knowledge

    Technology Used:
  • HTML5/CSS3
  • jQuery
  • Google Chrome API
  • Hipchat API
